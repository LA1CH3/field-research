Why this works

Using weight instead of hue for keywords is very much in the Solarized spirit — it reduces the total number of "chromatic voices" competing for attention. Now you have just 7 accent hues instead of 8, and the palette feels more grounded in the equipment aesthetic. The bold weight gives keywords enough presence to guide your eye through control flow without introducing a color that doesn't belong.

The dusty rose for numbers still provides warmth to balance the cool teal/cyan/green tones, but it's the only pink in the palette — which makes it feel intentional rather than random.
